How QTM Works

Every rand tells a story.

QTM bridges the gap between corporate CSR budgets and deserving NPOs — automating compliance, verifying impact, and removing every friction point between intention and action.

01

Browse the verified NPO catalogue

Filter by province, sector, BEE SED category and Section 18A eligibility. Every NPO is independently verified against DSD registers and bank-confirmed.

02

Select a Needs Card and fund in minutes

Costed, specific funding requests with clear deliverables. Know exactly what your rand achieves before you commit — no vague proposals.

03

Compliance pack generated instantly

Branded BEE SED certificate, Section 18A tax receipt, and compliance record — auto-generated the moment payment confirms. No chasing documents.

04

NPO delivers and uploads impact proof

After programme completion, NPOs upload photos and beneficiary data — visible directly in your dashboard and ready for your board report.
